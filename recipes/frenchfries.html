French Fries

Description

Deep fried potatoes prepared by cutting potatoes usually in even strips and frying them in oil.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash potatoes
  2. Cut potatoes in thin strips
  3. Preheat oil
  4. Fry potatoes in oil until they get a golden hue
  5. Take potatoes out of oil, let them rest and dry of any excess oil
  6. Season with salt to your liking
