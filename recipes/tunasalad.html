Tuna Salad

Description

A tuna salad rarely contains actual salad but it's delicious nonetheless. It can contain other vegetables though.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Corn
  • Scallions
  • Goat cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Remoulade sauce

Steps

  1. Open the can of tuna and place it on a plate.
  2. Chop tomatoes, scallions and goat cheese in small pieces, add them to the plate.
  3. Add the Remoulade sauce and mix together all the ingredients.
  4. Serve!
