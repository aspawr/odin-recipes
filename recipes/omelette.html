Omelette

Description

An omelette is made of whisked whole eggs to which you can add salt, pepper, onions, tomatoes, cheese and butter

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 medium tomato
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Crack and add the eggs in a bowl, whisk them and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan and once done add the eggs.
  3. Add chopped onion and tomato and let cook.
  4. Once cooked place them on a plate and add cheese to your liking.
